Web Series

Va Noi

Description

Va Noi is the latest up-and-coming YouTube web series, focusing on sharing interesting cultural picks via conversations with contemporary Viet personalities. Each guest would sit down and answer questions about what they have been reading, watching, listening to as of late.

Client

Va Noi

Scope

Brand Identity

Year

2021

We want to highlight the “human connection” that the creators of the series wish to communicate. Therefore, we crafted Noi Sans to be used for the brand's messages, with rough edges and handsy details. The handwritten quality of the font heightens the connection between the guests as well as between the guest and the viewers.

To express the show's dynamic and colorful personality, a color scheme that includes 4 color combinations are provided to be used interchangeably among applications. Moreover, we also created a set of bespoke stickers that surrounds the show's topic as well as the guests' hobby/interest.

Putting all the components together creates Va Noi's vocal outward expression as a love letter to pop culture. We also designed a wide array of digital applications such as cover images, social post templates, video thumbnails, so on.
