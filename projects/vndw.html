Events

VietNam Design Week 2022

Description

I had the opportunity to work along the Behalf team on the brand identity of Viet Nam Design Week 2022, a series of design-related talks and competitions across multiple disciplines. Together we devised a concept based on this year's theme, developed a key visual system and a set of guidelines to help with the event's collaterals, both on print and digital media.

Client

Designed by VietNam

Scope

Brand Identity

Year

2022

This year, Viet Nam Design Week 2022 bears the theme of “Beyond Expectations”—a reflection of their effort to bring about changes, both to the Vietnamese design scenes, and specifically their conventional identity designs in the previous years.

Inspired by the progress of metamorphosis in nature, we came up with a key visual system that resembles the image of a cocoon—a set of random invisible points in space that are connected via loose threads. These threads will then burst from their original positions, tearing the “cocoon” apart. The system employs typography as its main and only device, which helps convey the message clearly and successfully.

We made use of creative coding for this project by building a bespoke algorithm that can generate & export vector-based graphics based on our desired key visual structure. The results can then be incorporated into design files, giving designers (of both Behalf's team and VNDW's in-house team) the tool they need to design quickly, easily, and consistently.

The result is a unique & coherent system of event graphics across print & digital platforms. This not only helps express the theme of “Beyond Expectations”, but also brings attention and recognizability from event participants and fellow creatives.
