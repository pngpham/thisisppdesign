Dear Diary

Description

Dear Diary is a fun & casual photography project, taking a glimpse into the mind of a coming-of-age teenage girl. This character was portrayed through beauty shots in 3 different sets , each representing a distinct trait: rebellious, romantic, yet reflective.

Scope

Photography

Year

2020

I was heavily inspired by beauty & conceptual shots in the K-Pop (Korean pop) industry. I collected the images that inspired me most and 4 songs that can perfectly encapsulate the spirit of the photoshoot, which outlines the mood & feel of the whole project.

Mood Playlist

Chapter 1: Rebellious

Chapter 2: Romantic

Chapter 3: Reflective
