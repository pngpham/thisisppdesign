Illustration

Dalat Art Print

Description

In 2022, Dalat is recognized by UNESCO as a city of arts, specifically music. To celebrate the creative synergy of the city, Behalf team has worked alongside with Pho Ben Doi: Art Connects Us to create an artful set of merchandises for a local art fair.

Client

Dalat City

Scope

Illustration

Year

2022

The end goal of the project is to create an abstract artwork that represents Dalat city. In order to do so, we investigated several subject matters in the city, from ancient French architecture to the natural sceneries. With the help of Pho Ben Doi, we managed to come up with 4 illustrations, each representing a different side of Dalat: Domaine de Marie, sunflower field, Dalat University, and Hoa Binh theater.

After adding in some more details, the final illustration was adapted into various merchandises, including a set of two ceramic coasters and an A4-sized art print. We also created some miscellaneous paraphernalia items to be included in the brand such as stickers, paper bag, and so on.
