Republish's Lucky Envelopes

Description

Following the indispensable Vietnamese tradition of asking and giving lucky words during Tet, and also as a nod to our recent exhibition "Republish: Typography As…" at The Nutshell Saigon, we created a set of five envelopes that encloses the well wishes from our family at Behalf Studio to the people we love. The designs use the open-source typefaces of Behalf Studio's Republish project, combining a variety of glyphs to illustrate a graphical pentaptych, celebrating Tet typographically.

Scope

Print Design

Year

2021

This gift set pays homage to our Asian origin by referencing the image of a callighraphic work on red paper by Vietnamese calligraphy masters (ong do), with the text backbone of the designs outline the kind wishes dedicated to our friends and partners:

Duyên

Destiny

Phúc

Happiness

Lộc

Luck

An

Safety

Ý

Fortune

We utilize each Republish font's glyph set to visualize ubiquitous Tet item such as cherry blossom flower, apricot blossom flower, five-fruit tray, just to name a few. When put alongside, the five artworks are linked together via a bounding box, indicating the entirety of the gift set as well as its meaning behind.

The final outcome is riso printed on eco paper, adhesive-free & plastic-free. The packaging is printed on traditional red paper, meticulously sourced & crafted so as to bring out the best quality of the Tet gifts.

In celebration of the holiday spirit, we also updated our website to match the joyful synergy, wishing everyone a happy new year to come.
